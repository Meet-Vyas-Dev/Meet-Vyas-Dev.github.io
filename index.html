My Interests

During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to explore and work extensively in several domains, out of which I have described the three main broad categories below.

Physics

Interested in cosmology, mathematical general relativity, and gravitational collapse. I am deeply passionate about time-domain astronomy, with specific interests in light curve generation, transient detection, gravitational waves, and their integration within the broader framework of multi-messenger astronomy.

Computer Science

Interested in scheduling algorithms, artificial intelligence and machine learning applications, as well as data structures and algorithms (DSA), linear and integer programming, operations research, and constraint optimization problems. I am also keenly interested in computer shaders, cybersecurity, and full-stack web development.

Mechanical

Interested in understanding mechanical systems, aeronautical applications, solar PV cells and associated power generation, robotics, automation, CAD/CAM, and induction heating applications in Rotary Friction Welding.
